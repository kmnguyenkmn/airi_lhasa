Lhasa Apsos v.s. Shih Tzus

While there are may others breeds that resemble Lhasa Apsos, Shih Tzus are the closest to being a mini clone. I still can't tell them apart unless they were sitting side-by-side each other. I always told others who didn't know what Lhasa Apsos are, they are slightly larger versions of the Shih Tzu. Then it immediately clicks in their brain and agree that Airi resembles a bigger Shih Tzu. Below is a data table setup to (maybe) help others understand what exactly the difference and similarities between the two. Please keep in mind that this website doesn't have all the exact details, but the American Kennel Club is a great resource to learn more about Lhasas and Shih Tzus.
